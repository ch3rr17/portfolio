Mood Music Analyzer

Select from top moods or select the mood you're feeling today. This app will analyze your mood, play music according to your mood, and view images that coincides with your mood.

Stack Used: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap CSS, AngularJS and NodeJS

APIs Used: Spotify (https://developer.spotify.com/web-api/); Unsplash Images API(https://unsplash.com/developers)
